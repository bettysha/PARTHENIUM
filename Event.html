Event

The inaugural meeting for the book entitled, “Biological Control of Parthenium Weed in Ethiopia” was held in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia on July 23, 2024, at Haile Grand Hotel. The purpose of the meeting was to introduce the book to potential users in Ethiopia and facilitate its widespread use in classrooms and in the field.

The book assembles research-based information generated over seventeen-year period by USAID-funded project on the invasive weed, parthenium. The meeting brought together scientists, development workers, Ministry of Agriculture representatives and University instructors working on parthenium in different regions of Ethiopia.
